FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the router provided for free?
Yes, a high-speed dual-band Wi-Fi router is provided for free with all our long-term plans. For monthly plans, a small refundable deposit may apply.
How long does installation take?
We pride ourselves on quick service. Installation is typically completed within 24-48 hours of your request.
Are there any hidden installation charges?
No hidden charges! Our pricing is completely transparent. Any required installation fees are communicated clearly upfront before you commit.
What happens if I cross my data limit?
Our unlimited plans truly have no data limits. For limited data plans, your speed will simply be reduced to a slower tier (FUP) until the next billing cycle, without any surprise overage charges.
